HOW THEMES WORK

Each theme uses 8 colours. Here's what each does:

Background — the main page background. Warm cream or cool white for light; near-black for dark.
Surface — cards and modals. Slightly lighter/different from background.
Sidebar — left panel. Between background and surface in brightness.
Text (ink) — all body text. Must have strong contrast against your background.
Accent ⭐ — the star colour. Header bar, buttons, highlights. Make it bold and vibrant.
Secondary — complements accent. Doesn't have to be gold — try teal, pink, lime!
Muted text — labels and secondary info. Like ink but at ~50% intensity.
Border — lines between elements. Barely visible — just slightly off from background.
💡 TIPS
  • Start with the accent. It drives everything else.
  • Light themes: Use warm or cool tinted backgrounds — not pure white.
  • Dark themes: Never use pure black (#000). Try #080810 or #0d0d0d instead.
  • Contrast is king. Ink vs paper should be very different. Check the preview!
  • Border ≈ background + tiny nudge. Too dark = cluttered. Too light = invisible.
  • Muted ≈ ink at half strength. For dark themes with white text, use a mid-grey.
